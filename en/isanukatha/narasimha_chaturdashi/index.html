Narasimha Chaturdashi

[Weitergeleitet von சிவகுமார் sivakumar]
🌹 HAPPY NARASIMHA CHATURDASHI! 🌹

Today was the Holy day, when the Supreme Lord, Shrī Narasimhadeva descended to the world to protect His pure devotee Shrī Prahalāda Maharaj.🙏

Shrī Prahalāda Maharaja was an exalted and pure devotee of Shrī Hari, who had unflinching faith and devotion towards The Lord. His demonic father Hiranyakashipu tortured Him so much for that, but Shrī Prahalāda did not lose any faith in Shrīman Narayana, but in fact His love and devotion grew more for The Lord. After many tests and finally being impressed by His devotion, Lord Vishnu assumed His most ferocious form, to destroy Hiranyakishipu. Lord Naraharīshvara fought Hiranyakashipu and tore open His intenstines with His transcendental nails. He then wore his intestines as a garland around His neck and drank the blood of Hiranyakashipu.😱

The Lord is extremely tolerant and liberal even if you offend Him, but one little bit of offense towards His devotees and thats it, He can never tolerate it.😡

Through this pastime may we all understand that simply by having undeviated faith and devotion in The Lord, we will be protected from all forms of evil at all times. May Prahalāda Maharaja bless us with such immense faith and pure devotion towards The Lord. Pure devotion means to only want to be in The Lord’s devotional service without wanting anything in return birth after birth.

That is Bhakthi, if not it is Business!😜

“I offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Nrsimhadeva, the source of all power. O my Lord who possesses nails and teeth just like thunderbolts, kindly vanquish our demonlike desires for fruitive activity in this material world. Please appear in our hearts and drive away our ignorance so that by Your mercy we may become fearless in the struggle for existence in this material world. May there be good fortune throughout the universe, and may all envious persons be pacified. May all living entities become calm by practicing Bhakti Yoga, for by accepting devotional service they will think of each other’s welfare. Therefore let us all engage in the service of the supreme transcendence, Lord Sri Krsna, and always remain absorbed in thought of Him.”
- Srimad Bhagavatam 5.18.8-10

May He vanquish our sinning propensities and thus purify our existence so as to make us eligible to go back to Godhead!🙏

Shrī Mayapur Nrsimhadev ki JAI!🙏
Shrīla Bhaktha Prahalāda Maharaj ki JAI!🙏
Srīla prabhupāda ki JAI!🙏
